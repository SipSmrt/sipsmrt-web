About Us

We are Cole Khanjari, Matt Emerson, and Tommy Bird: three students from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute who care about hydration.

Our interest in the company began with our individual fitness journeys, where we recognized the importance of water and keeping yourself fully hydrated. With strong backgrounds in Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science, we put our heads together to think of a perfect solution.

This was the birth of SipSmrt.

The Product

Our product is a smart water bottle attachment that's versatile enough to be used with essentially any water bottle. Using weight sensors, we calculate and transmit the amount of water you've consumed and track it in a mobile app.

This mobile app, which is in simultaneous development for iOS and Android, will show your hydration goals, progress, and history. It will also remind you periodically if you are to fall behind these goals, or perhaps leave your water bottle behind.

Our scientific approach uses backed research based on biometric data, climate, age, and fitness level to give you an ideal hydration quota to strive for - with accomodations for non-water beverages as well!
